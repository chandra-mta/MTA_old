Time History of the HRC Background

The HRC background is dominated by the effects of charged particles (see memo by Juda). We extracted shield rates from dataseeker database, and plot agaist time. To avoid an effect of the Earth's radiaton belts, data with a geocentric distance larger than 80,000km is used. Although dataseeker data are logged in 5 min average, we convert it into one day average. The shield rate is count/sec, and time unit is day starting at a launch date (DOM).

For more detail anaylsis of HRC Background, please go to High Resolution Camera Stowed Background Study page.
